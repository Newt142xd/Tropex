ABOUT TROPEX

At Tropex, we specialize in exporting fresh fruits and herbs from Colombia to international markets.

We work closely with trusted growers to ensure consistent quality, reliable supply, and efficient logistics. Our focus is on building long-term partnerships through transparency, traceability, and high product standards.

From farm to destination, we connect Colombian producers with global markets, delivering freshness, reliability, and value in every shipment.

WHAT IS OUR SERVICE?

QUALITY SOURCING

We work directly with trusted growers to source premium fresh fruits and herbs that meet international quality standards.

SELECTION & PACKING

Each product is carefully selected, packed, and handled to preserve freshness, consistency, and shelf life throughout the supply chain.

EXPORT & LOGISTICS

We manage the export process with reliable logistics solutions, ensuring timely deliveries and full compliance with international requirements.
